Do you want to Join Afterlife?

Are you looking to join a Minecraft Content Creator SMP similar to Hermitcraft? Well look no further! The AfterLife SMP applications for Season 6 are open.

  • You produce quality Minecraft videos on a consistent and regular basis on your YouTube channel.
  • You are at least 16 years old when applying.
  • You will produce content on AfterLife SMP alongside with other content creators consistently.
  • You will only take part in one "vanilla" SMP like experience for content creation, as in you couldn't be a part of a server like Hermitcraft or TrulyBedrock and then be a part of AfterLife. A server that offers a unique experience like the Life series, OneShot, and others would be fine. If you do get accepted and are currently already a part of a "vanilla" SMP, you would unfortunately have to make a decision to leave the other server.
  • An application video is no longer required, but it is highly recommended! For making an application video, we recommend the following:
    1. This should be a representation of you and your content (personality and editing style)
    2. You showcasing your survival or creative world.
    3. If you consider yourself a builder, we would expect to see your builds.
    4. If you consider yourself a redstoner, we would expect to see your contraptions.
